# 📜 Aethelgard Character Design Guide
**Art Style**: WoW-Comic-Style (Stylized, Hand-Painted, Silhouette-Focused)

## 🎨 Overarching Design Principles
* **Proportions**: Heroic and slightly exaggerated. Hands, feet, and shoulders are approx. 10-15% larger than anatomically correct to increase readability from the third-person perspective.
* **Textures**: No photo-realistic materials. Everything is "painted." Shadows and highlights are baked directly into the texture.
* **Silhouettes**: Every culture must be distinguishable by its outline (e.g., Dwarves = square, Elves = slim/tall, Skjaldar = massive upper body).

## 🌍 The Cultures in Detail

1. **Imperials**
* **Vibe**: Discipline, heritage, order.
* **Silhouette**: Upright, symmetrical, athletic.
* **Colors**: Blue, gold, white, polished steel.
* **Features**: Short, neat hair. Armor featuring eagle wings or sun symbols.
2. **Skjaldar**
* **Vibe**: Storm, ferocity, honor in battle.
* **Silhouette**: V-shape (extremely broad shoulders), slightly forward-leaning, aggressive posture.
* **Colors**: Dark red, brown, dark iron, fur-grey.
* **Features**: Wild beards, braids, rune tattoos. Lots of leather and heavy fur.
3. **Zahiri**
* **Vibe**: Mysticism, starlight, sand magic.
* **Silhouette**: Slender, graceful, flowing transitions through loose fabrics.
* **Colors**: Turquoise, gold, warm desert-yellow.
* **Features**: Dark skin tones, eyes with a slight glow. Clothing with astronomical patterns.
4. **Rock Dwarves**
* **Vibe**: Resilience, blacksmithing, stone.
* **Silhouette**: Square, low center of gravity, massive.
* **Colors**: Dark copper, granite-grey, soot-black.
* **Features**: Beards with metal rings, coarse facial features. Massive plate armor.
5. **Feynir**
* **Vibe**: Nature spirits, speed, wolves.
* **Silhouette**: Appears very agile, asymmetrical armor parts (e.g., only one shoulder pauldron).
* **Colors**: Forest green, autumn gold, bark brown.
* **Features**: Amber eyes, war paint in natural colors, animal trophies (teeth, claws).
6. **Light Elves**
* **Vibe**: Arrogance, ether, starlight.
* **Silhouette**: Very tall, extremely narrow, appearing almost floating.
* **Colors**: Silver, violet, pearlescent, light glass.
* **Features**: Long, straight hair (white/platinum), glowing eyes (white/blue). Filigree armor.
7. **Night Elves**
* **Vibe**: Forbidden magic, abyss, survival.
* **Silhouette**: Wiry, lurking (like a predator).
* **Colors**: Black, dark violet, poison green, obsidian.
* **Features**: Pale to grayish skin, dark tattoos, often hoods and masks.
8. **Nordfolk**
* **Vibe**: Cold, will to survive, barrenness.
* **Silhouette**: Similar to Imperials, but more "thickly" bundled.
* **Colors**: White, ice blue, coarse linen-grey.
* **Features**: Frost in the beard, pale skin, functional clothing without pomp.
9. **Southbound – NEW**
* **Vibe**: Trade, knowledge, diplomacy, subtle wealth.
* **Silhouette**: Loose, relaxed, more "civilian" than military.
* **Colors**: Purple, emerald green, brass, silk-white.
* **Features**: Neat appearance, rings, jewelry. Clothing is often multi-layered and signals prosperity. Faces appear more intellectual or cunning than warlike.
* **Equipment**: Scholar robes, decorated leather armor for travelers, heavy volumes or map scrolls on the belt.
10. **Deep Dwarves**
* **Vibe**: Knowledge, ancient runes, depth, melancholy.
* **Silhouette**: Similarly compact as Rock Dwarves, but less "angular." Their posture is often slightly bent, as if they were constantly brooding over old scriptures or peering into dark tunnels.
* **Colors**: Deep azure blue, copper (tarnished), dark anthracite.
* **Features**:
    - **Beards**: Often decorated with glowing runestones or parchment scrolls.
    - **Skin**: Paler than Rock Dwarves, almost like alabaster, as they rarely see sunlight.
    - **Eyes**: Often possess a soft, inner glow (rune magic).
* **Equipment**:
    - Combination of heavy robes and fine chainmail.
    - Their armor is engraved with glowing runes that shimmer in the dark.
    - Often carry mechanical lanterns or large crystals on the belt.
