# 📜 The Chronicles of Aethelgard
# 📜 Project Aethelgard: Lore & System Reference
This document bundles the central pillars of the world of Aethelgard, its powers, and the mechanical architecture of the RPG system.

## 🌌 1. Cosmology
Aethelgard is a world in an eternal struggle between divine order and primordial corruption. While the eight gods of the Pantheon protect civilization, nature, and spirit, the four Adversaries strive to return the world to its elemental origins (Ash, Ice, Madness, Hunger).

## 🌑 3. The Adversaries (Corruption)
The Adversaries offer shortcuts to power. Every gift (Taint Gift) is a double-edged sword: it grants enormous bonuses but demands a permanent price in the form of flaws (Penalties).

🐺 **Fenrisul** (The World Devourer)
* Opponent: Solan (Hunger vs. Light)
* Gift: Bestial Wrath (`KK +3`, `MU +1`)
* Flaw: `CH -2`, `KL -2`. Bestial features, speech devolves to growls.

❄️ **Hela-Ka** (The Frost Queen)
* Opponent: Yldra (Stasis vs. Growth)
* Gift: Heart of Ice (`KO +3`, Cold Resistance)
* Flaw: `IN -2`. Total emotional coldness; loss of empathy.

👁️ **Vorthos** (The Lie Weaver)
* Opponent: Mimir (Will-o'-wisp vs. Insight)
* Gift: Whispering Runes (`Mana +15`)
* Flaw: `Perception -4`. Reality is overlaid by a thousand lying voices.

⚔️ **Surtr** (The World Fire)
* Opponent: Valeria (Destruction vs. Honor)
* Gift: Bloodfire (`Swords/Blunt Weapons +3`)
* Flaw: `Self-control -5`. Retreat or mercy is impossible in bloodlust.

* **Xal-Atath** (The Empty Hand): Unnatural thieving skills, but luck leaves the bearer.
* **Nidhogg** (The Root Gnawer): Resistance to poison and pain, but the body begins to rot.
* **Skoll** (The Sun Hunter): Become one with shadows, but blinds the user in sunlight.
* **Malakor** (The Eternal Standstill): Skin as hard as stone (high armor), but limbs stiffen.

## ⚙️ 4. Character System
### Primary Attributes
Each character is defined by eight base stats:
* **Mental**: Courage (`MU`), Cleverness (`KL`), Intuition (`IN`), Charisma (`CH`)
* **Physical**: Dexterity (`FF`), Agility (`GE`), Constitution (`KO`), Strength (`KK`)

### Derived Stats
* HP (Health Points): Capacity for physical damage.
* Mana: Energy for magical actions.
* Favor: Religious energy (Max. 25). Consumed for divine miracles.
* Taint Level: Measures the degree of corruption (0 to 100).
